Space Beings

Animation 🚀

Type

Digital art

Tools

Illustrator, Animate

Timespan

December 2020

In a distant galaxy, a twisting celestial tree stands tall, embracing the universe with outstretched branches. At its trunk is a dormant guardian. In its wood resides a family of amorphous beings. They spend their perpetual night gathering crystals, observing faraway planets, and playing among the stars, living at peace in the little space they call home.
