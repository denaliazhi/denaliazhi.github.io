Dreamscape

Type

Digital art

Tools

You might be familiar with the idea of "counting sheep to fall asleep," but have you ever wondered where the sheep go? 🐑

In this illustration, a curious little girl decides to follow her furry friend. They wander along a cool stone path through lush vegetation glistening with dew. Eventually, they come across a cryptic wooden sign: dreamland. What is this mysterious destination? You'll have to sleep to find out.
