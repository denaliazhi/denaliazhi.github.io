Amenuveve

Catalogue design

Type

Graphic design

Tools

InDesign, Photoshop

Timespan

1 week, June 2020

Amenuveve is a nonprofit dedicated to the Ghanaian village of Woadze Tsatoe. One of its programs is a batik center where local women learn to craft traditional printed fabrics that can be sold to customers around the world.

As the designer for Amenuveve, I created a catalogue to showcase these goods to a U.S. audience. My work included editing photos and writing copy to align with the brand identity.
