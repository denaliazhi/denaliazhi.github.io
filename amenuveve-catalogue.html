Amenuveve

Catalogue design

Type

Graphic design

Tools

InDesign, Photoshop

Timespan

1 week, June 2020

Amenuveve is an international nonprofit dedicated to serving the Ghanaian village of Woadze Tsatoe. One of its intitiatives was to empower women in the village through the founding of a small batik enterprise, which crafts and sells traditional block-printed products. I helped create a catalogue to showcase these artisan goods to U.S. consumers.
