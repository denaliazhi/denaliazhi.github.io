The Journey

Digital Tools Final Project

OUART-UT 823 in the NYU Course Catalogue

One of my Pinterest-inspired travel dreams is to take a cross-continental road trip in a vintage volkswagen van. Through this project, I've made that dream a (virtual) reality. Follow the journey of my pink VW across a series of fictitious landscapes, from the rusty sands of the "Sahararizona" at sunrise to the purple mountain majesty of a camp site at sundown.
